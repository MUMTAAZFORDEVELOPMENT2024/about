VISION.
To be an excellent organization in empowering Tanzanian's youths, women and children, to acquire better education, having better health and conserving their environment.

MISSION
To empower and promote Tanzanian youths, women and children, to acquire better education, having better health, conserving their environment, and to have access to necessary resources, by providing and facilitating, consulting, training, research, advocacy and counselling.


OBJECTIVES AND GOALS OF MUMTAAZ
The following are the objectives of Mumtaaz:-
1-To promote quality education provision.
2-To rise awareness in fighting against different diseases, like HIV, TB, Malaria, Mental health, e.t.c.
3- To empower youths and women in alleviation of poverty.
4- To create awareness about gender based violence (GBV) in the society.
5-To establish projects on environmental conservation, like planting trees, and cleanliness programs.



OUR CORE VALUES.
1-Sincerity- we believe in working with whole heart and have sense of humanity and fear of God The Almighty. The work should be undertaken with integrity, openness, honesty and professionalism.
2-Perfection- we believe in doing our works in right ways and attaining excellence.
3-Team work- we believe in working as a team, with joining hands together for the community.
4-Transparency- we believe we should be responsible to each other and to the people and organisations with whom we engage and strive to make our work transparent and accountable.
5-Volunteerism- we believe in working and accomplishing our activities voluntarily, and we open the doors for others to volunteer to serve our community.
6-Service- we believe in providing services to targeted groups (students, teachers, youths and women), will give us credit and comfort to our hearts as far as being happy.
7-Empowerment- we believe in independence and long term solutions to problems through sustainable projects.
